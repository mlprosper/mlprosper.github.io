Strange error message this is...  Therefore notify Box we must..mmmm?

It's always a good idea to have someone proof your error messages to detect wording that may obscure an error rather than clarify it.

Besides being poorly worded, this error message doesn't clearly address a few key issues:

  • When was the folder deleted?
  • Who is the "collaborator?"  Who deleted the file?
  • If the folder was moved, why are the original and final folder locations the same?
  • What am I supposed to do with the file on my desktop?

A little more information would be helpful, as well as a clear explanation of what to do next.
