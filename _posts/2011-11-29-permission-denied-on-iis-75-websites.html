Along with the problem of not serving LESS files, I’ve also had issues in IIS with permissions and giving file access to the proper account.  Here are a few links that have helped:

http://perezj.blogspot.com/2010/07/iis-75-windows-server-2008-r2-access.html

http://forums.iis.net/t/1156692.aspx

Whatever you do, don’t just give carte blanche access to the IUSR group or another group.  Take the time to read the articles and give as little access as possible.
