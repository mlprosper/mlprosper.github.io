'{{unknown}}' comments: true --- I've recently started using TextMate for development on my Mac and needed some bundles for ruby gems like HAML  and SASS.  After some looking on Google and reviewing the various forks for the HAML and SASS bundles, I settled on the bundles from Philippe Huibonhoa.  He updates the bundles frequently and it seemed have the most features added to the default bundle.  Here's his github address: https://github.com/phuibonhoa and the bundles:
  • Rails - http://github.com/phuibonhoa/ruby-on-rails-tmbundle
  • Ruby - http://github.com/phuibonhoa/ruby-tmbundle
  • Shoulda - http://github.com/phuibonhoa/ruby-shoulda-tmbundle
  • HAML - http://github.com/phuibonhoa/handcrafted-haml-textmate-bundle
  • Sass - http://github.com/phuibonhoa/ruby-sass-tmbundle
  • JavaScript - http://github.com/phuibonhoa/Javascript-Bundle-Extension
  • CTags - http://github.com/phuibonhoa/tm-ctags-tmbundle
